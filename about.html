About

Hello! My name is Nick Harper. I'm 18 years old, and I currently live in Castle Rock, Colorado.

I'm just an individual that enjoys creating useful websites and tools for others to use.

I'm a self-taught web developer, and I specialize primarily in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. Though I am open to learning new things in order to create better products.

I also have experience in UI design, though I am more skilled in the back end of things.

Brief History

I began coding in 2008. Though, being so young, I had troubles understanding how to do anything with simple HTML and CSS, and I had no motivation to learn. Later in 2010, I found my self in the same position, but this time I was more determined.

Since then, I have been constantly learning and building new things. I have been using my free time to make coding a hobby I enjoy, and soon to be a career.

If you are interested in my work, please feel free to contact me.
