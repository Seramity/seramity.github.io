What is Streme?

Streme is a platform that will connect all your music into one place, including your Last.fm Scrobbles, "stremes", and communities. The goal for Streme is to create a useful platform for those with a passion for music, and to provide the tools for them to organize their interests.

Features

Profiles & Community

Streme will be utilizing Last.fm for user profiles. This means that linking your Last.fm account will grant access to a personalized profile with all your scrobbles and library that you have built. This profile can be viewed by you and other users on Streme.

On Streme, you can add other users as friends where you can message them, recommend music to them, and much more.

Libraries

With the inclusion of the Last.fm API, Streme will also be using the music database within it. Therefore, users can easily explore through thousands of artists, albums, etc.

Stremes

Got a collection of music you think is dope and want to share it? Well, you can do dso with stremes: a playlist of songs, albums, etc. Stremes can be shared to everyone across the globe!
