Kudobit - PHP

A new haven for programmers

Kudobit is a free service that allows programmers to share snippets of code to other individuals around the world.

Our main focus is to allow people to share their work. Whether if they want to share their code, or to receive feedback on a problem they are stuck on.

Mission

Create an easy platform for developers to share their unique work with others.

Technologies

Kudobit was created using PHP, no frameworks. Data and all that was managed with MySQL (phpMyAdmin).

Kudobit was made based off of a small PHP authorization framework i built prior to development.
