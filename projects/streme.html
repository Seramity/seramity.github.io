Streme - PHP

Easily connect with the music you care about.

Streme is a platform that connects all your music into one place, including your Last.fm Scrobbles, "stremes", and communities.

Mission

The goal for Streme is to create a useful platform for those with a passion for music, and to provide the tools for them to organize their interests.

There are already websites and apps that perform the same function as Streme, but many of them, including Last.fm, have been striped of features that are crucial for these types of platforms. Last.fm used to be the whole package in terms of user freedom and creativity, but has now recently worsened after the beta update which had removed many of its qualities, and has resulted in a decline in its userbase.

With that in mind, Streme will run off of the Last.fm API so that the user can link their Last.fm account and sync their library. In addition, Streme will bring the community back to life by including forums and stremes (playlists) for users to share.

Technologies

Streme.in was created with PHP (Slim Framework). Data is managed with mySQL.

Last.fm API for music catalog and user authentication.

When Rate Your Music finally gets an API, Streme will be using RYM for ratings.
